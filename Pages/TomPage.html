Application of Computing within Education

Computing has lots of benefits in education and allows students to learn more content and learn more efficiently

Some of the general benefits include:

  • Improved ommunication between Teachers and student
  • Improved critical thinking as multiple sources to learn information
  • More engaging learning for students with stimulate motivation
  • Easier access to information

All of these benefits help students to have a better education and experience with while are school due to the help of Computers

Computer and technology have specific benefits to some students:

  • Computers allows for remote learning and reduces to requirement to meet in person. This benefits people who live have disability or can't travel.
  • Computers helps teacher to teach students in different ways and allows people how have learning need to learn effectively.
  • Computer allows all student with internet access to access same information reducing costs.

However, they are some problems that technology has on education

  • It requires students to have some sort of technology such as a laptop which not all student can afford.
  • Computer can be a distraction if incorrectly implemented
  • It makes it easier for students to cheat
  • Not all information online is correct and can cause some students to learn incorrect facts
